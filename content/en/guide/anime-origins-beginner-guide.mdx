<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering Traits and Rerolls">

Traits are randomized modifiers applied to units upon summoning. They can drastically alter a unit's effectiveness by providing buffs to range, damage, attack speed, or even special utility effects. This **anime origins beginner guide** recommends focusing on traits only after you have secured a solid team of Mythic or Legendary units.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Reroll Tokens">
    Earn tokens through daily missions, stage completions, or event rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Top-Tier Units">
    Only use reroll tokens on units you plan to keep long-term (Mythics).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Aim for 0.1% Traits">
    Watch for the ultra-rare traits. These not only provide massive stat boosts but often come with exclusive visual auras.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Lock and Save">
    Once you hit a high-tier trait like "Godly" or "Unique," stop rerolling and move to your next unit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Trait Tier | Rarity | Typical Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Godly** | 0.1% | Massive Damage + Special Animation |
| **Ethereal** | 0.5% | Significant Range and Speed boost |
| **Superior** | 2.0% | Moderate Stat Increase |
| **Basic** | 90%+ | Minor Stat Increase |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Warning">
Trait rerolling is highly addictive and resource-heavy. Do not spend all your tokens trying to get a 0.1% trait on a unit you will replace in a week.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Strategic Combat and Placement">

Success in Anime Origins isn't just about having the strongest units; it's about where you put them. Most maps feature winding paths that allow for "chokepoint" strategies. Placing units with Area of Effect (AOE) damage at corners or loops maximizes their efficiency.

| Unit Type | Best Placement | Primary Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **AOE DPS** | Corners / U-Turns | Hit multiple enemies at once |
| **Single Target** | Start of the track | Finish off high-health bosses |
| **Slow/Stun** | Middle of the track | Keep enemies in DPS range longer |
| **Buff/Support** | Center of unit clusters | Boost effectiveness of multiple towers |

Strategic depth increases as you unlock units with active abilities. Some Mythic units require manual activation of their ultimate skills, which can clear an entire screen of enemies if timed correctly during a boss wave.

<Callout type="info" title="Strategy Note">
Early in a match, prioritize placing one strong "carry" unit and upgrading it fully rather than placing many weak units. Quality often beats quantity in the early waves.
</Callout>

</Section>
