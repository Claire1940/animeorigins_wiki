<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Farming: Infinite Mode vs Raids">

Once your account transitions out of the beginner phase, you must decide how to allocate your active playtime. Both Infinite Mode and Raids are excellent for high-tier **anime origins gem farming**, but they demand completely different team compositions and strategies.

Infinite Mode is a test of endurance. Enemies scale continuously, meaning you need units with exceptional damage scaling and percent-based or crowd-control abilities. Raids, on the other hand, are highly coordinated, fast-paced encounters that require rapid economy setup and precise placement to counter specific boss mechanics.

| Farming Aspect | Infinite Mode | Raid Encounters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Time Commitment** | Long (30-60 minutes per run) | Short (10-20 minutes per run) |
| **Player Coordination** | Solo-friendly, low coordination | High coordination (Multiplayer recommended) |
| **Primary Failure Point** | Scaling enemy HP overpowering defenses | Boss abilities destroying key placements |
| **Resource Rewards** | Gems & repeatable upgrade materials | Gems & exclusive evolution items |

  </Card>
  <Card title="Raid Strategy">

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Units for Faster Runs">

Farming gems efficiently is not just about surviving; it is about how fast you can clear waves. Reducing the time spent per run drastically increases your hourly gem yield. To achieve this, you must optimize your units using the Trait and Evolution systems.

Evolving a unit significantly boosts its base damage, range, and attack speed, allowing you to clear early waves much faster. Additionally, rolling high-tier traits like those focused on Damage or Cooldown reduction will turn your primary carry units into absolute powerhouses.

| Trait Focus | Stat Bonus | Best Unit Type | Farming Impact |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Damage** | Increases damage per hit | Primary AoE carries & boss killers | High (Clears waves faster) |
| **Cooldown** | Reduces delay between attacks | Slow, stun, or support units | High (Keeps crowd control active) |
| **Range** | Expands targeting area | Hill units & map-wide attackers | Medium (Allows flexible placement) |
| **Secret Trait** | Massive multi-stat boost | Evolved Secret or Mythic units | Extreme (The ultimate farming roll) |

<Callout type="success" title="Success">
A single fully evolved unit with a high-tier Damage or Cooldown trait can easily carry you through early-to-mid Story stages solo, allowing you to speed-run maps and accumulate hundreds of gems in record time.
</Callout>
