<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Loop">

To systematically improve your account, follow this structured progression path. This sequence is designed to maximize your resource efficiency and prevent common beginner mistakes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Summon Your Starter Units">
    Head to the summon area and use your initial currency to pull your first units. Aim for a mix of rarities to fill out your deployment slots. Do not worry about pulling Secret units immediately; a solid rare or epic unit is more than enough to handle early waves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Form a Balanced Lineup">
    Assemble a team that covers all essential combat roles. A balanced team requires inexpensive ground units for early waves, long-range hill units for consistent damage, and utility units for slow or freeze effects.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Progress Through Story Mode">
    Clear Story Mode stages consecutively. Story Mode is the best way to earn experience, unlock new features, and gather the base resources needed for character progression. Avoid farming the same early stage repeatedly if you can push forward.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Unit Placements">
    During battle, place your units near corners, bends, or overlapping lanes. This ensures that enemies remain inside your units' attack ranges for the maximum possible duration, drastically increasing your total damage output.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prioritize In-Battle Upgrades">
    Instead of spamming the map with weak, low-level units, focus your cash on upgrading your core damage dealers. A single fully upgraded unit is almost always more effective than several unupgraded ones.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Placement Warning">
Always check a unit's range indicator before confirming its placement. Placing a short-range unit too far from a path bend will waste valuable attack cycles.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Traits and Rerolls">

Traits are passive bonuses applied to your units that can completely change their combat effectiveness. Rolling the correct trait is a crucial step in moving from an **anime origins noob to pro** competitor.

| Trait Focus | Stat Bonus | Best Suited For | Reroll Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Damage** | Increases raw attack power | Main DPS carries | High |
| **Cooldown** | Reduces time between attacks | Support and fast attackers | High |
| **Range** | Expands attack circle | Hill units and AoE attackers | Medium |
| **Secret Trait** | Unique cutscene & massive stats | Evolved or Secret units | Keep |

<Callout type="info" title="Reroll Strategy">
Never waste your limited reroll tokens on temporary starter units. Save them until you acquire Mythic or Secret units, such as Yuta Okkotsu, who will remain in your active team permanently.
</Callout>
