<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trait System and Stat Rerolling">

Traits and stats can make or break your units in high-level content. Rerolling allows you to customize your units' performance, transforming a standard Mythic into an absolute powerhouse.

### Trait Tier List and Effects

The trait system features various tiers, ranging from basic utility boosts to game-breaking secret traits. The ultimate goal for any primary DPS unit is to secure a secret trait.

| Trait Name | Rarity | Main Effects | Recommended For |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Immortal** | Secret (0.1%) | Restores base power, instant-kills nearby enemies, knocks back/slows | Main DPS (Yuta) |
| **Ascendant** | Mythic | Substantial damage and attack speed scaling | Any DPS Unit |
| **Overseer** | Mythic | Deals bonus true damage on every attack | High-Speed Units |
| **Hustler** | Rare | Increases range and income generation | Utility / Support |

### Stat Rerolling Priority

In addition to traits, you can reroll individual stats (Damage, Range, and Attack Speed) from grade F up to SS.

| Stat | Priority | Target Grade | Impact |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Damage** | High | S / SS | Maximizes base scaling and skill output |
| **Range** | Medium | S / SS | Increases coverage, crucial for slow-hitting units |
| **Attack Speed** | High | S / SS | Lowers attack cooldowns, essential for status appliers |

<Callout type="success" title="Reroll Strategy">
Set your auto-reroll settings to stop on **Immortal** or **Ascendant** traits. When rolling stats, prioritize securing an S or SS grade in Damage first, as this scales best with unit evolution.
</Callout>
