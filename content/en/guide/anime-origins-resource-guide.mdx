<Section icon={Swords} title="Trait Reroll System and Probabilities">

Traits are passive modifiers assigned to units upon summoning. These modifiers can drastically alter a unit's performance by boosting damage, attack speed, range, or utility. The trait reroll system allows players to customize their units by replacing unwanted traits with stronger alternatives using reroll tokens.

The distribution of traits is heavily weighted, with the most powerful traits having extremely low obtainment rates. Players must manage their reroll tokens carefully, focusing only on units that will remain in their primary lineup for a long duration.

| Trait Tier | Roll Probability | Key Effects |
|---|---|---|
| Godly | 0.1% | Massive damage multiplier and range expansion |
| Unique | 1.0% | High attack speed increase and cooldown reduction |
| Rare | 9.0% | Moderate damage and range improvements |
| Common | 89.9% | Minor stat boosts to health or attack speed |
