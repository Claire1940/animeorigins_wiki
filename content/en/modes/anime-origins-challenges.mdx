<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Story Mode Progression">

Story Mode serves as the foundation for all **anime origins challenges**. Each stage presents a unique path that enemies follow toward your base. Your goal is to prevent them from reaching the exit by placing units along the route. In the early stages, focus on cost-efficient units that can handle groups of enemies. As you progress into later worlds, you will encounter enemies with higher health pools and special resistances that require a more balanced team.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Unit Placement">
    Identify the first major turn or intersection on the map. Place a low-cost, fast-attacking unit here to catch early waves while you build up your economy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Economy Management">
    Do not spend all your gold on new placements immediately. Focus on upgrading your core damage dealer to reach their "AoE" (Area of Effect) or "Multi-hit" milestones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Support Deployment">
    Once your main damage is stable, place support units that can slow down enemies or buff your attackers' range and damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Map-Specific Strategy">
    Some maps have "Hill" spots for ranged units and "Ground" spots for melee. Ensure your team composition matches the available slots on the map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Wave Preparation">
    Save enough currency to max out your strongest unit before the Boss wave. The final wave in Story Mode often features a significant jump in enemy health.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| World Level | Difficulty | Enemy Traits | Recommended Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **World 1** | Easy | Basic Grunts | Focus on single-target damage |
| **World 2** | Medium | Fast Runners | Prioritize high attack speed |
| **World 3** | Hard | Shielded Units | Use AoE and multi-hit units |
| **World 4** | Expert | Regenerating Bosses | High DPS and slow debuffs |

<Callout type="info" title="Important Note">
Clearing Story Mode stages is the only way to unlock the higher difficulty versions of those maps, which provide significantly better rewards for the same time investment.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Infinite Mode: Pushing the Limits">

Infinite Mode is where the most dedicated players spend their time. Unlike Story Mode, there is no "win" condition here; the waves continue until your base health reaches zero. The enemies scale infinitely in health, making it a true test of your team's sustained DPS. To excel in these **anime origins challenges**, you need units with high scaling potential and traits that reduce cooldowns or increase range.

    - Essential for late waves

  <Card title="The Crowd Control">

In Infinite Mode, placement is everything. You want your units to have the maximum possible "uptime" on the enemies. This means placing them in the center of "U" turns or loops where their range can cover multiple sections of the track. As the waves progress into the hundreds, even a 1% difference in trait efficiency can determine whether you survive another five waves.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Optimizing Units for High-Level Challenges">

To conquer the hardest **anime origins challenges**, simply having rare units isn't enough. You must optimize them through the Trait and Evolution systems. Traits provide passive bonuses that can drastically change a unit's performance. For example, a "Secret Trait" might offer a 0.1% chance to trigger a massive damage burst, which is game-changing in a Raid or deep Infinite run.

Evolution is the next step in power. By collecting materials from Raids and Story Mode, you can evolve eligible units into their advanced forms. These evolved units usually have better stats, new attack animations, and higher upgrade caps.

| Trait Focus | Best For | Reroll Priority | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Damage %** | All Attackers | **High** | Increases total DPS |
| **Cooldown %** | Support/Slow | **High** | More frequent attacks |
| **Range %** | Hill Units | **Medium** | Better map coverage |
| **Secret Trait** | Main Carries | **Keep** | Unique visual/stat buff |

<Callout type="tip" title="Optimization Strategy">
Do not waste your Reroll Tokens on "Common" or "Rare" units. Save them for "Mythic" or "Secret" units that you plan to keep in your team for the long term. Evolution should also be prioritized for units that provide the most utility across all modes.
</Callout>
