<Section icon={Swords} title="The Raijin Thunder God Raid Walkthrough">

The Raijin Thunder God raid is currently the most challenging encounter in the rotation. The boss is heavily inspired by traditional Japanese Shinto mythology, utilizing lightning drums (Tomoe) and summoning a demon lightning wolf known as Raiju. Players must adapt to fast-paced electrical hazards and coordinate their positioning to avoid wiping.

During the first phase, Raijin hovers in the center of the arena, surrounded by a barrier powered by eight lightning drums. Attempting to damage the boss directly during this phase is highly inefficient. Instead, players must focus their attacks on the individual drums while dodging localized lightning strikes marked by blue circles on the ground.

As the fight progresses into the second phase, Raijin summons Raiju, the lightning demon wolf. According to legend, this creature hides in children's belly buttons, prompting the boss to strike the ground with thunderbolts to wake it. In the raid, Raiju targets the player with the highest threat level, leaving a trail of electrified ground that deals damage over time.

<Callout type="tip" title="Positioning Strategy">
Keep the boss centered while your ranged DPS units spread out along the outer edge of the arena. This prevents Raijin's chain lightning attacks from bouncing between multiple party members, minimizing the healing burden on your support units.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Destroy the Tomoe Drums">
    Focus all single-target attacks on the eight outer drums to disable Raijin's barrier. Avoid standing directly under the drums, as they emit periodic electrical pulses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Intercept the Raiju Summon">
    When the lightning wolf appears, your off-tank or main utility unit must draw its aggro. Lead the beast away from the main group to prevent it from cluttering the arena with electric pools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Survive the Global Strike">
    At 50% health, Raijin unleashes a room-wide ultimate attack. Seek shelter inside the protective barriers generated by your support units or dodge at the precise moment of the flash.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Final Burst">
    Once the shield is down and the summons are defeated, unleash all ultimate abilities. Use attack-buffing skills to maximize damage during this brief vulnerability window.
  </Step>
</Steps>
