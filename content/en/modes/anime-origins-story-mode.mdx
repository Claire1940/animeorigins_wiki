Navigating through the campaign requires a structured approach to avoid hitting progression walls. Following a optimized path ensures that your resources are spent efficiently, allowing your team to stay ahead of the enemy scaling curve.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Core Team">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Chapter 1 and 2 Immediately">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enhance Starter Gear">
    Equip your active roster with the best available gear from Chapter 2. Enhance all weapons to at least level 10 to boost your team's attack power. This stat boost is critical for clearing the timed stages in Chapter 3.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Farm Awakening Shards">
    Before entering Chapter 4, spend your daily energy on awakening stages. Awakening your primary damage dealer unlocks their passive ability, which significantly increases their combat effectiveness in boss fights.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Conquer Chapter 5 Nightmare">

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Target} title="Boss Strategies & Tactics">

Every chapter concludes with a unique boss encounter that introduces specific mechanics. Ignoring these mechanics will result in team wipes, even if your power level exceeds the recommendation.

| Boss Name | Chapter | Core Mechanic | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Sand Guardian | Chapter 2 | Sandstorm shield reduces incoming damage by eighty percent | Use water skills to dissolve the shield and stun the boss |
| Soul Reaper | Chapter 3 | Targets the unit with the lowest health for an instant kill | Keep healer skills ready to top off health before the cast |
| Fleet Admiral | Chapter 4 | Summons artillery strikes that cover the entire battlefield | Move units to the designated safe zones marked in blue |
| Void Emperor | Chapter 5 | Regenerates health when dealing damage to shielded targets | Avoid using shield-generating units; rely on raw healing |

To defeat the Void Emperor in the final chapter, you must manage your skill cooldowns carefully. The boss enters a vulnerable state after casting its ultimate ability, during which it takes double damage. Save your highest-damaging skills for this specific window.

<Callout type="warning" title="Boss Rage Mode">
When a boss's health drops below thirty percent, they enter a rage state, increasing their attack speed and damage output. Save your ultimate abilities to burst them down quickly during this phase.
</Callout>
