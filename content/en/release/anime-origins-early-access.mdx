To tackle the hardest content in the game, you must optimize your units through the Trait and Evolution systems. These mechanics permanently enhance your characters' combat capabilities.

### Trait Reroll Priorities

Traits provide passive stat boosts to your units, modifying damage, range, or attack speed. The rare **Secret Trait** features a 0.1% roll rate and activates a unique visual cutscene upon acquisition.

| Trait Type | Stat Focus | Reroll Priority | Best Suited For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Secret Trait** | Multi-Stat Boost | Keep | Permanent endgame carries |
| **Damage** | Attack Power | High | Primary damage dealers & boss killers |
| **Cooldown** | Attack Speed | High | Support units & crowd controllers |
| **Range** | Attack Radius | Medium | Hill units & sniper characters |

### Evolution Progression Pathway

Evolution turns eligible units into advanced combat forms with upgraded models, enhanced attack effects, and superior base statistics.

| Step | Phase | Action Required | Expected Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | Eligibility | Obtain an evolvable unit | Unlock the evolution path in the UI |
| **2** | Gathering | Farm specific challenge modes | Accumulate required evolution materials |
| **3** | Selection | Select the correct unit copy | Confirm desired traits are preserved |
| **4** | Completion | Submit materials in the menu | Acquire the upgraded advanced combat form |
