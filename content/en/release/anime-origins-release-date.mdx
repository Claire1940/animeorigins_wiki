<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trait Rerolls and Evolution Mechanics">

To maximize the combat effectiveness of your roster, you must utilize the trait reroll and evolution systems. Traits are passive modifiers assigned to units upon summoning. These modifiers can drastically alter stats such as attack speed, range, and critical strike chance. The rarest traits have a spawn chance as low as 0.1% and feature custom visual effects that make your units stand out on the battlefield.

| Trait Name | Roll Chance | Stat Modifier | Visual Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Swift | 10.0% | +15% Attack Speed | None |
| Range Master | 5.0% | +20% Attack Range | Subtle aura |
| Demonic | 1.0% | +50% Damage, -10% Range | Red glowing eyes |
| Origin | 0.1% | +100% Damage, +25% Speed | Full character aura |

Evolution takes your units to the next level by upgrading their rarity, stats, and skill sets. Evolving a unit requires specific materials collected from daily challenge dungeons. This process is resource-intensive, so players must prioritize which units to evolve first based on their current team needs.
