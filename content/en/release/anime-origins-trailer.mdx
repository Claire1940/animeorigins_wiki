<Section icon={Map} title="Game Modes and Progression Structures">

The core gameplay loop revealed in the **anime origins trailer** footage revolves around three primary pillars: Story Mode, Infinite Mode, and Raids. Progressing through these modes is essential for gathering the resources required to summon and evolve your roster. Story Mode serves as the foundation, teaching players map layouts and enemy pathing, while Infinite Mode tests the limits of a team's sustained damage output.

| Mode | Primary Objective | Best For | Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Story Mode** | Defend the base through set waves | Initial progression/Unlocking | Beginner |
| **Infinite Mode** | Survive scaling waves indefinitely | Resource farming/Testing | Moderate |
| **Raids** | Defeat high-health boss encounters | Evolution materials | Hard |
| **Multiplayer** | Coordinate with allies on shared maps | Group farming/Social | Variable |

Each mode requires a different tactical approach. In Story Mode, cost-efficient units are prioritized to handle early-game pressure. Conversely, Infinite Mode necessitates units with high scaling potential and massive range to deal with the overwhelming enemy density of later waves.

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy Note">

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trait System and Optimization">

To further differentiate units, Anime Origins includes a robust Trait system. When a unit is summoned, it receives a random trait that modifies its base statistics. Players can use "Reroll Tokens" to try for better traits. The **anime origins trailer** previews highlighted a "Secret Trait" that not only provides massive stat boosts but also triggers a special visual aura when the unit is placed on the battlefield.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Core Carries">
    Focus your reroll resources on units that will stay in your team long-term, such as Secret or Mythic damage dealers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Evaluate Current Traits">
    Assess if the unit's current trait aligns with its role. For example, a damage dealer benefits most from "Damage" or "Cooldown" traits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Reroll Tokens">
    Use tokens to cycle through traits until you hit a high-tier modifier like "Godly" or the elusive "Secret Trait."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Lock and Test">
    Once a desirable trait is found, test the unit in Infinite Mode to see the impact on its total damage-per-second (DPS).
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Trait Type | Effect | Priority | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Damage** | Increases raw attack power | High | All DPS units |
| **Cooldown** | Reduces time between attacks | High | Fast attackers |
| **Range** | Increases attack radius | Medium | Hill/Support units |
| **Secret** | Massive all-stat boost + Aura | Maximum | Main Carry |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Do not waste reroll tokens on Rare or Epic units. These units are usually replaced quickly as you progress into the mid-game, making the investment inefficient.
</Callout>

</Section>
