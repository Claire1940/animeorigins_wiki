<Section icon={Shield} title="The Trait System and Reroll Strategy">

One of the most complex aspects of the **anime origins updates** is the trait system. Traits are permanent passives applied to units through rerolling. These can range from simple stat boosts to game-changing mechanics. The **Immortal** trait is the current pinnacle of the system, boasting a 0.1% roll chance. It provides a massive power restoration effect and an automatic knockback/slow that triggers once per match after the 10th wave.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Trait NPC">
    Navigate to the upgrade area in the main lobby and select the Trait Reroll option.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Target Unit">
    Choose a Mythic or Secret unit. It is highly recommended to only spend reroll tokens on units you plan to evolve.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Set Auto-Roll Filters">
    Use the UI to select desired traits like **Immortal**, **Overseer**, or **Ascendant** to prevent the system from skipping them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Stats">
    After securing a top-tier trait, use stat rerolls to aim for **SS Damage** or **S Range** to maximize the unit's potential.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Beyond the Immortal trait, players should look for **Overseer** for true damage or **Ascendant** for overall stat boosts. The **Hustler** trait is excellent for farm units, as it increases income generation, allowing for faster upgrades during difficult story maps like Karakura Town.

| Trait Name | Rarity | Main Effect | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Immortal** | 0.1% | True damage / Knockback / Slow | Secret Units (Yuta) |
| **Overseer** | Rare | Bonus True Damage per hit | High Attack Speed Units |
| **Hustler** | Common | Increased wave income | Farm Units (Bulma) |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Evolution, Ascension, and Raids">

The endgame of **anime origins updates** revolves around the **Evolution** and **Ascension** systems. Evolution requires specific "Remnants" and secondary units to transform a base Mythic into its ultimate form. For example, Yuta evolves into his Cursed Lover state, gaining the **Copy** ability and a devastating energy beam that strikes five times in a wide line, dealing 300% damage.

Ascension takes this a step further by allowing players to spend resources to increase **Crit Chance** and **Crit Damage** permanently. This is essential for tackling the **Seven Deadly Sins Raids**. In these raids, players can earn exclusive units like **Ban**. The drop rates are tiered based on difficulty, making high-level coordination vital.

| Raid Tier | Difficulty | Ban Drop Rate | Other Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Tier 2** | Medium | 1% | Rare Remnants, Reroll Tokens |
| **Tier 3** | Hard | 3% | Legendary Remnants, Ascension Shards |

**Evolution Steps for Yuta:**
- **Step 1**: Reach max level on base Yuta unit.
- **Step 2**: Collect all required Remnants from Karakura Town Legend stages.
- **Step 3**: Use the Evolution NPC to combine materials.
- **Step 4**: Unlock the **Copy** passive and energy beam active ability.
