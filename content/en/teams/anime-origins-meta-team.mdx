<Section icon={Target} title="Evolution and Progression Meta">

A unit's base form is rarely enough to tackle the hardest Raids. To truly optimize your **anime origins meta team**, you must utilize the Evolution system. This process transforms eligible units into significantly stronger versions, often granting them entirely new abilities or visual effects.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Eligible Units">
    Check the unit progression menu to see which characters have an "Evolution" path. Not all units can be evolved, so plan your investment accordingly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Mode-Specific Materials">
    Evolution requires resources found in Story Mode, Infinite Mode, and specific Raids. You will often need duplicate units or unique items dropped by bosses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select the Best Base Unit">
    Ensure the unit you are evolving has a high-tier Trait (like Damage or Cooldown). The Trait usually carries over or provides a better foundation for the evolved form.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Evolution">
    Submit the materials in the evolution interface. Your unit will be reset to level 1 but will have vastly superior base stats and upgrade potential.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Material Type | Primary Source | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Story Shards** | Clearing Story Stages | Base evolution for Mythic units |
| **Raid Essences** | Completing High-Tier Raids | Required for Secret unit upgrades |
| **Infinite Tokens** | Reaching high waves in Infinite | Purchasing evolution catalysts |
| **Duplicate Units** | Summoning Banners | Often required as "food" for the main unit |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Do not evolve every unit you find. Evolution materials are scarce in the early game. Prioritize your main DPS first, as they provide the most significant return on investment for clearing harder content.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trait Rerolling and Optimization">

Traits are the hidden modifiers that separate a good unit from a meta-defining one. In Anime Origins, traits can boost Damage, Range, or Cooldown. For the most elite **anime origins meta team**, landing a "Secret Trait" (0.1% chance) is the ultimate goal, as it provides a massive stat boost and a unique activation cutscene during battle.

| Trait Name | Bonus Effect | Best For | Meta Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Damage** | Increases total attack power | All DPS units | **High** |
| **Cooldown** | Reduces time between attacks | Support and CC units | **High** |
| **Range** | Increases attack radius | Hill and Sniper units | **Medium** |
| **Secret Trait** | Massive multi-stat boost | Your primary carry (Yuta/Meliodas) | **God Tier** |

<Callout type="success" title="Reroll Tip">
Only use your reroll crystals on units you plan to keep permanently. Spending resources to get a "Godly" trait on a starter unit is a waste of progression speed.
</Callout>

</Section>
