<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Team Building & Placement Guide">

Once you have assembled a balanced roster, executing the proper deployment order during a match is critical to survival. Follow this step-by-step progression route to secure your defense on any map:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Deploy Your Early-Wave Defender">
    Place a cheap, fast-attacking unit near the entrance or the first major turn. This ensures you clear the fast-moving weak enemies without leaking base health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Set Up Your Economy Units">
    As soon as the initial defense is stable, place your money-generating units. Start upgrading them early to snowball your cash flow for the mid-game waves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Position Your Main Carry">
    Deploy your primary damage dealer (such as Meliodas or Starrk) at a central junction or bend. Ensure their attack range covers multiple lanes or overlapping paths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Add Crowd Control and Support">
    Place slowing or stunning units just before your main carry's kill zone. This groups enemies together, allowing your AoE attackers to hit more targets simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Max Out Upgrades Strategically">
    Instead of placing dozens of weak units, focus on maxing out the upgrade paths of your core damage dealers. One fully upgraded unit is far more effective than several low-level ones.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Co-Op Placement Tip">
When playing multiplayer challenges, coordinate with your teammates so that one player focuses on early-wave defense and economy, while others prepare high-cost endgame damage units.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Units with Traits and Rerolls">

Traits provide massive percentage-based boosts to a unit's base stats, including damage output, attack range, and cooldown reduction. Getting the right trait on your main carry can completely transform your combat performance.

Use the comparison table below to determine which traits to keep and which to reroll:

| Trait Focus | Combat Effect | Best Unit Type | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Damage Boost** | Increases raw damage per hit | Primary DPS, boss killers | **High** |
| **Cooldown Reduction** | Decreases time between attacks | Fast attackers, status appliers | **High** |
| **Range Expansion** | Increases total targeting area | Hill units, sniper characters | **Medium** |
| **Secret Trait** | Massive multi-stat buff + cutscene | Your absolute best carry unit | **Keep (0.1% Rate)** |

<Callout type="info" title="Trait Matching Strategy">
For units with slow, high-damage attacks, prioritize Cooldown Reduction traits to smooth out their damage delivery. For units that already attack quickly, raw Damage Boost traits offer the best scaling.
</Callout>
