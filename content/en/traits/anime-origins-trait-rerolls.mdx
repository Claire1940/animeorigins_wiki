Traits are divided into several categories, each targeting a specific combat attribute. Choosing the right focus for your unit depends on their natural kit. For instance, a unit with a massive Area of Effect (AoE) but a slow attack speed benefits more from Cooldown reduction than a minor Range increase.

| Trait Type | Primary Effect | Best For | Reroll Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Damage** | Increases raw power per hit | Boss killers, Single-target carries | **High** |
| **Cooldown** | Reduces time between attacks | Crowd control, Status applicators | **High** |
| **Range** | Expands the attack radius | Hill units, Support units | **Medium** |
| **Balanced** | Modifies multiple core stats | Versatile Story Mode units | **Medium** |
| **Secret** | Massive multi-stat boost | Endgame Mythic and Secret units | **Keep** |

When evaluating **anime origins trait rerolls**, you must consider the unit's "Scaling." A unit like Meliodas (Assault Mode) scales exceptionally well with Damage because his multi-hit abilities multiply the effectiveness of every point of power added. Conversely, units designed to slow or stun enemies need Cooldown traits to keep the debuff active permanently.
