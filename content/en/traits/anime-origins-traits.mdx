<Section icon={Target} title="How to Reroll: A Step-by-Step Trait Optimization Guide">

Rerolling traits requires careful planning to avoid wasting valuable tokens. Follow this structured process to maximize your resource efficiency and secure high-tier traits for your main roster.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Reroll Tokens">
    Obtain reroll tokens by completing daily challenges, clearing infinite mode milestones, or purchasing them from the in-game shop using gems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select the Target Unit">
    Navigate to the character inventory and select a high-tier legendary or mythic unit. Ensure the unit is not locked before attempting to modify its stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Inspect Current Traits">
    Check the unit's existing trait and reference the tier list. If the unit already possesses an Epic or Legendary trait, evaluate whether you have enough tokens to risk a reroll.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Reroll Process">
    Use the reroll button to consume one token and roll a new trait. The system will display the new trait along with its corresponding stat adjustments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Decide to Keep or Roll Again">
    Determine if the rolled trait aligns with the unit's role. If you roll a mythic trait like Divine, stop immediately and lock the unit to prevent accidental rerolls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Accidental Rerolls">
Always double-check that your lock status is active on units with mythic traits. Accidental rerolls cannot be undone, and support teams cannot restore lost traits.
</Callout>

For players seeking to optimize multiple units, it is recommended to distribute your reroll tokens across your main team rather than dumping all tokens into a single character. A balanced team with Epic traits will outperform a team with one Mythic unit and four traitless units.

</Section>
