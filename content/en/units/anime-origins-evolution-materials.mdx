<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Evolution Process">

Once you have secured the necessary **anime origins evolution materials**, the actual process of evolving a unit is straightforward but requires careful confirmation to avoid wasting resources on the wrong duplicate.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select the Base Unit">
    Navigate to your unit inventory and select the character you wish to upgrade. Ensure the unit has reached the maximum level required for evolution (usually Level 80 or 100).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Verify Material Counts">
    Open the "Evolve" tab within the unit's profile. Check that you have the exact number of materials required. The interface will highlight missing items in red.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Trait Synergy">
    Evolution preserves the unit's current Trait. If you have a "Godly" or "Secret" trait, ensure it is on the unit you are about to evolve. It is much harder to reroll traits on evolved units due to higher costs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm Transformation">
    Click the "Evolve" button. A short cinematic will play, and your unit will be replaced by its advanced form. Note that the unit's placement cost in-game may increase slightly after evolution.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Note on Placement Cost">
Evolved units often have a higher initial placement cost but offer better "Value per Dollar" in terms of damage-to-cost ratio during the mid-game waves.
</Callout>
