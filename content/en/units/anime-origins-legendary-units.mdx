<Section icon={Target} title="Trait Reroll System and Best Buffs">

Traits are passive modifiers assigned to units upon summoning. These traits can drastically alter a unit's performance, adding percentage-based stat increases, range extensions, or speed boosts. The trait reroll system allows players to customize their **anime origins legendary units** to fit specific strategies. While common traits offer minor boosts, securing a rare or unique trait can turn an average unit into an unstoppable force. Rerolling requires specific tokens obtained through daily quests, challenges, or event rewards.

When rolling for traits, the math behind percentage-based buffs becomes highly relevant. A 50% damage buff applied to a high-level legendary unit yields a massive increase in raw attack power compared to the same buff on a lower-tier character. Therefore, players should prioritize rerolling traits on their primary damage dealers before focusing on support units.

| Trait Name | Rarity | Stat Buff | Meta Rating |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Godly** | 0.1% | +50% Damage, +10% Range | Excellent |
| **Demonic** | 0.5% | +35% Damage, +15% Speed | Great |
| **Sniper** | 1.0% | +25% Range | Good |
| **Swift** | 2.0% | +20% Attack Speed | Good |
| **Heavy** | 5.0% | +15% Damage, -5% Speed | Average |

Securing a "Godly" or "Demonic" trait is rare, but the investment is worthwhile for endgame content. These top-tier traits also unlock unique visual effects and attack animations, adding a cosmetic reward to the gameplay progression.

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Evolution Guide">

Evolution is the process of upgrading your base legendary characters into their mythic or ascended forms. This process unlocks new skill trees, enhances base statistics, and often changes the unit's visual effects and attack patterns. Evolving a unit requires a combination of duplicate characters, gold, and specific evolution materials farmed from challenge stages.

To farm these materials efficiently, players must tackle daily dungeons that rotate their rewards based on the day of the week. Preparing teams with elemental advantages against these dungeons makes the farming process much faster and more reliable.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Max Unit Level">
    Train your base legendary unit to its maximum level using experience potions or by deploying them in standard stages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Materials">
    Farm the required elemental shards and catalysts from the weekly challenge dungeons and material raids.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Acquire Duplicates">

  <Step num="4" title="Perform Evolution">

Managing inventory space is also important during this process. Keep track of duplicate units and lock key characters to prevent accidentally using them as experience fodder or selling them for gold.
