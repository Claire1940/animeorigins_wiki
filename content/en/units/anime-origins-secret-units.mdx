<Section icon={Target} title="How to Summon and Optimize Secret Units">

Obtaining **anime origins secret units** requires a combination of luck and strategic resource management. The summoning system is the primary gateway, but optimization through traits is what truly unlocks a unit's potential.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accumulate Gems">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Banner Rotations">
    Wait for a banner that features the specific secret unit you want. Pulling on a generic banner reduces your chances of getting a specific "Featured" secret.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger the Secret Cutscene">

  <Step num="4" title="Roll for Secret Traits">

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Secret Trait Advantage">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Endgame Strategies for Secret Unit Users">

Once you have secured your **anime origins secret units**, your focus shifts to team synergy and placement strategy. Even a Secret unit can fail if placed poorly or if the team lacks economy support.

### Placement and Economy Strategy

1.  **Economy First**: Always start matches with economy-focused units to ensure you can afford the expensive upgrades required for Secret units.
2.  **Corner Overlap**: Place units like Yuta Okkotsu at bends or corners where their AoE can hit the same enemy group multiple times.
3.  **Buff Stacking**: Use support units to increase the Range and Damage of your Secret carry. The multipliers from support units are calculated *after* the Secret unit's base stats.

| Strategy Component | Recommendation | Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Early Game** | Place cheap Mythics for initial waves | High |
| **Mid Game** | Maximize Economy units (Farms) | Critical |
| **Late Game** | Deploy and max-upgrade Secret units | High |
| **Placement** | Focus on "U-turns" and "Loops" | Medium |
