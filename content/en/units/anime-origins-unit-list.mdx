<Section icon={Target} title="How to Evolve Your Units">

Evolution is the process of taking a base character from the **anime origins unit list** and transforming them into their "Ascended" form. This increases their level cap, unlocks new abilities, and significantly boosts their base stats. Evolution is not cheap; it requires a combination of evolution stones and specific fodder units.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Max Level Attainment">
    Before a unit can be evolved, it must reach the current level cap (Level 80 for most Mythics). Use EXP food obtained from the Training Grounds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Materials">
    Each unit requires specific "Elemental Orbs" and "Evolution Stones." These are farmed in the Daily Challenge maps which rotate every 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sacrifice Fodder">
    Higher-tier evolutions require you to sacrifice 3-5 units of the same rarity or lower. Ensure you aren't sacrificing units you need for other teams.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Ascension">
    Once all requirements are met, use the Evolution altar. This will reset the unit's level to 1, but its base stats will now be higher than the previous max.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Evolution Stage | Level Req | Material Cost | Stat Increase |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stage 1** | 40 | 10 Stones, 5 Orbs | **+25% All Stats** |
| **Stage 2** | 60 | 25 Stones, 15 Orbs | **+50% All Stats** |
| **Stage 3** | 80 | 50 Stones, 30 Orbs | **+100% All Stats** |

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
Ascension also unlocks a unit's secondary passive ability, which is often crucial for clearing the final waves of Infinite Mode.
</Callout>
