<Section icon={Map} title="Evolution Pathways and Material Gathering">

Evolution is the ultimate goal for any unit featured in an **anime origins unit showcase**. Transforming a base unit into its "Assault" or "Awakened" form significantly alters its placement cost and ability set. This process requires specific materials gathered from Story Mode and Raids.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Eligible Base Unit">
    Ensure you have the base version of the unit (e.g., Meliodas). Check the evolution menu to confirm the unit is eligible for an upgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Mode-Specific Materials">
    Enter Story Mode or Raids to collect evolution shards. Different units require different elemental or character-specific drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Leveling the Unit">
    Most evolutions require the base unit to reach a specific level threshold before the transformation can be triggered.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Transformation">
    Submit the materials in the Evolution Hub. The unit will transform, retaining its current level and trait but gaining a new model and enhanced skills.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Evolution Material | Primary Source | Drop Rate | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Character Shards** | **Character-specific Raids** | **Low** | Core evolution component |
| **Essence of Origin** | Infinite Mode (Wave 50+) | Guaranteed | Universal upgrade material |
| **Gold/Currency** | All Game Modes | High | Transaction fee for evolution |

<Callout type="info" title="Evolution Tip">
